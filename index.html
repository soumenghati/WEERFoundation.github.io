WEER Foundation
Young minds joining hands to bring change & achieve greater human heights.
We are a Waste Management, Environment, Education & Recycle Solutions focussed NGO
OBJECTIVES
Waste Management Solutions
Quality Education For Underprivileged
Services for The Backward and Under Priveliged
Promoting Innovation & Recycling Solutions
Vision
To EMPOWER underprivileged students and enabling them to live their life with DIGNITY and respect by acquiring quality education.
Mission
We aspire to lead and support a community that fosters a positive commitment to the environment by reducing waste and service to communities.

CURRENT PROJECTS - COMING SOON
Presently WEER Foundation has been doing research on various waste management, recycling solutions which are scienctifically sustainable and causes minimal harm to environment. WEER foundation is working for sustainability in urban and rural areas, which include the work like; plantation, saving water resources, waste management(recycling & reuse), employment and health issues in our country. We work on the projects like Finding Jugnu, Waste Besties and Khelta Bajpan which impact our societies in many ways in sustainability development. Sustainbility : “SABKA SATH SABKA VIKAS”
