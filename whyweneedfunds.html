Why We Need Funds?

The trust is a non-profitable organization and is working to provide good quality education to the underprivilidged children especially in rural areas. This trust has a clear vision to spread awareness about waste segregation, recycling methods, saving the environment and innovate new scientifically proven methods to manage waste and environment better. To provide quality education and spread awareness, the trust has to invest money to achieve the goals. The trust is committed to help underprivileged children with free education, uniforms and books.
