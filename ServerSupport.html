Waste Management Solutions

WEER Foundation is committed to the sustainable development goals which aim to achieve fundamental improvements in the approach to Solid and E-Waste Management in low-and middle-income countries, focusing in particular - the improved livelihoods and living conditions of the urban & poor.

WEER Foundation is executing Sustainable Waste Management Project with the given objectives:

 Spread awareness regarding the 3Rs - Reduce Reuse and Recycle.

 Generating employments for the villagers, unskilled workers and the individuals under poverty.

 Development of useful products from the waste generated and collected.
