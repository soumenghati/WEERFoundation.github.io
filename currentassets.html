Volunteer With Us

Be an Intern / Volunteer/ Team Member

Volunteering Opportunities

 Mentoring children on weekends.
 Organizing extra-curricular workshops.
 Conducting fund raising drives.
 Operational and Field Activities.


WEER Foundation offers a diverse range of volunteering/ intern opportunities. Through these, you can, not only contribute towards the betterment of the society & environment, but also undergo a transformational journey for yourselves. Whatever be your background or work experience is, if you have the passion and skills to make a difference Any hiring process include with the submission of a resume at: admin@weerfoundation.org followed by personal interview. At the end of the internship / volunteering assignment, interns have to submit a report and make a presentation to WTSW Foundation management which is duly certified.
